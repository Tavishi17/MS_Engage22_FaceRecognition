About

Mask Detector can ensure public safety by installing it at

In this project, the algorithm detects all the faces present in the input and then the mask detector model is run for all these identified faces. As an output, the detected faces have a rectangular box around them with the label stating the status of mask detection along with the accuracy. A gender-recognition model is also deployed and the label consists of the predicted gender as well.

Services

At Hospitals

Using this, hospitals can monitor if their staff is wearing masks during their shift or not. If any health worker is found without a mask, they will receive a notification with a reminder to wear a mask.

At Offices

This can be used in office premises to ensure that employees are maintaining safety standards at work. It can monitor employees who enter the premises without mask and send them a reminder to wear a mask.

At Schools

Using this, proper regulation of reuired guidelines can be ensured. For example, in chemistry lab mask is necessay due to the harmful emmisions, entry can be regularised using this application.
